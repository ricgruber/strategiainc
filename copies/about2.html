About Strategia

Our founders who are now lawyers met in law school & became friends over discussions regarding web design & development. They both have had a clear mission from the beginning; help companies make a difference in the hearts and minds of their audience by creating meaningful digital experiences.

Strategia's specialty is creating market disruptions by finding innovative solutions to traditional problems within existing business models.
